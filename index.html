Tribute to technology

Technology has always been something I enjoyed. Now it is something I truly appreciate. After the passing of my grandparents Eve Lindsey And Mario Medina the only thing I have left of them is the pictures, videos, and voicemails. This may not sound like much but its means the world to me.

Because of technology my last memories of them dont only have to be in my head. By the click of a button I can hear their laugh, I can listen to all the silly and heartfelt voicemails they left me, I can see their smile in every picture, and I can watch them dance in every video. So I wanted to make a tribute to technology and the brilliant inventors that made this possible over time.

In loving memory of Eve Lindsey and Mario Medina




Here's a timeline of some major technology inventions

  • 1870 - Thomas Edison develops phonograph, the first method of recording and playing back sound on metal foil.
  • 1875 - Alexander Bell patents the telephone.
  • 1966 - Robert H. Dennard of IBM invents dynamic random access memory (DRAM).
  • 1970 - Gordon matthews created the voicemail
  • 1883 - George Eastman invents plastic photographic film.
  • 1888 - Eastman Kodak created kodak, the simple single lens reflex camera that used film.
  • 1973 - Martin Cooper develops the first handheld cellphone (mobile phone).
  • 1980 - Japanese electrical pioneer Akio Morita develops the Sony Walkman, the first truly portable player for recorded music.
  • 1991 - The first webcam was created and was used to monitor a coffee pot in the computer science department at Cabridge University.
  • 1993 - IBM Simon released as a concept phone, the first smartphone.
  • 2003 - The mobile era began with the introduction to Android.
  • 2007 - The mobile era began with the introduction of the Iphone.
  • Currently Have access to Calls, texts, facetime, cameras, voicemail, air drop, transolator, AI, billions of websites and so much more.
